Non-Crimp Fabric

V2's non-crimp fabrics offer strength, structural integrity

Maintain the fabric's structural integrity and promote physical properties of fibers. Straight line construction promotes optimal physical properties with non-crimp architecture.

Full Product Description Features & Benefits
Full Product Description

The stitch-bonded, multi-axial construction of V2’s non-crimp fabrics prevent the degradation of fiber strength, providing superior structural integrity and longer-lasting performance. Straight line construction promotes higher tensile strength, maintains the fabric’s structural integrity, preserves its mechanical qualities (such as drape or stiffness), reduces lay-up time (faster wet-out) and cost (higher fiber volumes).

V-Lock® "no-run" Stitch Technology

Trusted technology prevents reinforcement fabrics from unraveling

A patented process that prevents frayed edges, running stitches and deconstruction.

Full Product Description Additional Features
Full Product Description

V2's patented V-Lock stitch technology is a breakthrough “no run” system that makes non-crimp multiaxial fabrics more stable by preventing frayed edges, running stitches and deconstruction. V-Lock eliminates many of the problems associated with traditional stitch matrices, including entangled stitches, frayed edges and fabrics falling apart when cut into complex shapes.

How it Works

Seam Tape, combined with NAC TAC Primer or NS97 Exterior Primer, protects wall-to-floor seams from water intrusion. Strataflex sheet membrane, applied with NAC TAC Primer or NS97 Exterior Primer, combats water penetration, structural and thermal movement and Moisture Vapor Transmission. For durability and full protection, Pre-Formed Corners are installed with SubSeal® liquid membrane. The entire area is then covered with SubSeal for a watertight finish. And to seal the deal, a urethane sealant is used to seal any termination points in the membrane system.

Additional Features
  1. Significantly reduce fabric waste
  2. Expedite fabrication processes
  3. Extend the longevity of pre-cut dry reinforcement materials
